Served 6 1/2 years in the Air Force as a Logistics Officer with experience in Air Transportation, Supply Chain Management and Acquisition stationed previously in the U.S., Europe and Asia. Currently, I am married to my wife Emma for going on 4 years. I separated from the Air Force last year and now work as a Senior Consultant for a defense contractor, Booz Allen Hamilton.
